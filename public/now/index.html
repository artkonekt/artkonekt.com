Vanilo

Release v2.0

Vanilo is our E-commerce platform for Laravel. The next release is a major one, v2.0 and is scheduled for June 2020.

Along with the Open Source v2.0 release, vanilo.cloud will be launched, which offers Vanilo as a managed E-commerce platform for Shop owners.

Check it out at vanilo.io.

Upriserz

Phoenix Platform

The Upriserz E-learning platform has received a major upgrade called "Phoenix".

The Phoenix project has been successfully launched and migrated on September 15, 2020.

The upgrade was a complete rework of the subscription management system. The admin area has been rewritten in Laravel Nova. The LMS has been "insourced", replacing the previous Thinkific based solution.

Red Carnation Hotels

Vuejs Rework

We're working together with Matter of Form on the major rework of the Red Carnation Hotels websites family.

The new system features an Umbraco backend and a modern, vuejs based frontend supplying a faster and smoother browsing experience.

The brand site goes live on the 1st of October, which will be followed by the unique hotel sites.
